What is Intellect?

Intellect, by definition, is a term used in studies of the human mind and is used to describe the brain's ability to come to conclusions about problems. At Intellect, we thought that the best way to showcase this was through jokes. The problem with social media is the negative environment that it is built on. Here, we strive to create a positive environment for the most humorous people. Our goal is to make you happy, whether it's puns, memes, or jokes of any kind. If you're having a bad day, this is the site to cheer you up, and if you're having a good day, this is the site to make it a great one.

A Message From the Creators

Everything that we do at Intellect links back to one thing: Happiness.
The goal when designing the site was to create a space that invokes users to display their positivity, creativity, and of course, Intellect.
